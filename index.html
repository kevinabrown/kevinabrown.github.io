Biography

Kevin A. Brown is a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Argonne Leadership Computing Facility (ALCF) at Argonne National Laboratory, a US Department of Energy national research laboratory. Kevin works in the area of large-scale high-performance computing (supercomputing) with his current research focused on Exascale Interconnect Performance Evaluation.

Kevin’s profile page at ANL is located here.
